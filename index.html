01 · About

Human-centered interfaces for spatial computing.

Jihyeon Lee is a Ph.D. student in the Graduate School of Culture Technology at KAIST, working in the Visual Cognition Lab. Her work spans controller-free 3D environments, gaze and microgesture interaction, VR teleportation, AR sticky notes, and multimodal interfaces for augmented reality.
